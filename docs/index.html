DataUtility

Modular pandas toolkit for cleaning, EDA, outliers, encoding, scaling, and feature engineering

Why this library exists

Most projects repeat the same tasks. DataUtility keeps these steps consistent and reusable.

What "safe" means here

Methods validate columns and types, avoid silent behavior, and support inplace=True/False.
